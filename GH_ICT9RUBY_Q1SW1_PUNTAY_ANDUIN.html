Alternative Rock

Alternative Rock, often referred to as "Alt Rock," is a diverse genre of rock music that emerged in the 1980s. It is known for its departure from the mainstream rock sound, incorporating a variety of influences, including punk, post-punk, and new wave. Bands in this genre often emphasize artistic expression, experimentation, and a rejection of commercialism.


Characteristics of Alternative Rock:

  • Influence from punk, post-punk, and new wave
  • Lyrics often focus on social issues and introspection
  • Distinct from mainstream rock
